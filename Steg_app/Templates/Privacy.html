Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Stego-Vault collects, uses, protects, and shares your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Email, username, encrypted passwords, and contact details.
  • Usage Data: Login times, IP addresses, and interaction logs (for activity tracking and security).
  • Encrypted Content: Messages, images, watermarks, and associated encryption keys.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide secure encryption, steganography, and watermarking services.
  • To enhance application performance and user experience.
  • To notify users of activities and important updates.
  • For analytics and platform improvement (without revealing private user content).

3. How We Protect Your Data

  • All sensitive data (like passwords, AES/RSA keys) are stored using secure encryption standards.
  • User sessions are protected using Django's built-in security mechanisms.
  • We restrict unauthorized access and log suspicious activities for investigation.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Information may be disclosed only:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect our rights or investigate misuse of the platform.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use basic session cookies for authentication and tracking login status. No third-party cookies or trackers are used.

6. User Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request data deletion (subject to security review).
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential communications.

7. Third-Party Services

Stego-Vault does not integrate with third-party services or APIs that access your private content.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be reflected on this page and major updates will be notified via your dashboard or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this policy, feel free to contact our support team at jayuj6028@gmail.com.
